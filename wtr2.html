CGI's expertise includes:

  • Digital transformation

With their extensive knowledge and forward-thinking approach, CGI helps organizations tackle complex challenges and seize new opportunities in today's fast-paced digital landscape.

Goals

Another goal was to improve my creative thinking skills. I aimed to enhance my creativity in generating innovative ideas and solutions for technical projects. Overall Reflection

Reflecting on my goals, I have made progress in enhancing my oral communication, creativity, and visual literacy skills. Participating in workshops, hackathons and seeking inspiration from diverse sources has improved my ability to generate innovative ideas.

Job Description

Overview
During my first work term, I contributed to the development of the CGI Experience Design System (EDS) by creating a React version of the existing Angular framework. In my second work term, I focused on designing a chatbot front end in Figma, which was utilized in two projects: one to assist EDS users in designing and creating pages and UIs more efficiently, and another for a file management system. I implemented these designs in both Angular and React. I also updated the current system to Angular 18 and Material 3, replaced legacy components, and incorporated design tokens to enhance consistency and maintainability.

Unique Aspects of the Job
As previously mentioned, one unique aspect of this job was that it was part of CGI's Women in STEM Excel (WiSE) co-op program, which offered many learning opportunities. In December, I participated in another hackathon, which provided a platform to apply my skills in a competitive and collaborative environment, further enhancing my problem-solving abilities. Throughout my time at CGI, I had the opportunity to interact and collaborate with a diverse group of professionals, including those with expertise in areas like machine learning and project management. This experience enriched my understanding and broadened my skill set.


Skills Required and Learning Experience

The WiSE Co-op Program at CGI requires a range of skills, including proficiency in front-end languages such as HTML, SCSS, Typescript, and JavaScript, and frameworks like Angular and React. Knowledge of server-side programming in Python and Java, as well as familiarity with version control systems like Git. Success in this role requires strong problem-solving abilities, attention to detail, excellent communication skills, and the ability to work both independently and collaboratively. I developed a strong foundation of skills through my academic projects using languages like C, Java, and Python. These projects significantly improved my teamwork and communication abilities. On the job, I further enhanced these skills and learned new ones, such as improving my attention to detail and self-teaching abilities, as I had to teach myself several new technologies.

Conclusions

My time at CGI has been very rewarding. I contributed to various projects, from helping implement the new React version of the CGI Experience Design System (EDS) to designing a front-end chatbot UI using Figma. These experiences enhanced my technical skills, problem-solving abilities, attention to detail, and communication skills. I also learned the importance of self-teaching new technologies. I participated in meaningful events like “Knitting and Crochet for a Cause,” where we created squares, combined them into a blanket, and donated it to those in need. We also had a second hackathon with all the co-ops, which was a great opportunity to collaborate, innovate, and apply our skills in a competitive yet supportive environment.


Overall, the WiSE Co-op Program provided a strong foundation for my career. The supportive environment and diverse learning opportunities at CGI have improved my abilities to tackle complex challenges and contribute effectively to any team.
